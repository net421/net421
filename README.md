### Near-Critical Network Digital Twin

Research-grade extension of a near-critical inventory-control benchmark into a networked supply-chain digital twin. The project compares run-to-failure, static safety-stock, local near-critical, and network-aware preventive threshold policies under stochastic disruptions.

Key result: preventive intervention value is strongest when physical criticality, economic criticality, and network concentration occur together. In delta-controlled digital-twin experiments, network-aware policy dominated the most near-critical regimes and benefits declined toward zero in relaxed regimes.

**Demonstrates:** Digital Twins • Monte Carlo Simulation • Operations Research • Network Science • Supply-Chain Resilience • Reproducible Research
